Elaine Fletcher Chapman provides confidential and convenient telephone consultations supported by occasional emails when needed. She works with artists concerning difficulties specific to the practicing of one's art, especially regarding perseverance, performance, and doubt. She often asks: “What longing is being left unattended? What is your heart's desire?”

“Midway on our life's journey, I found myself
In dark woods, the right road lost.”

˜Dante Alighieri/Robert Pinsky

She has been working with people during difficult times and periods of life transitions and transformation for over thirty years. She provides support and an attentive presence. Her expertise lies in her ability to envision positive solutions to nearly impossible situations. She holds hope in the face of hopelessness. She believes in a holistic approach, integration of mind, body, and spirit and the practice of living in the moment. She believes strongly in the possibility of change.
